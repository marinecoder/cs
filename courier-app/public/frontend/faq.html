Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services

General Questions

CourierDash is a professional courier and delivery service platform that provides fast, reliable, and secure package delivery with real-time tracking. We connect customers with verified couriers for same-day, next-day, and scheduled deliveries across the country.

Creating an account is simple. Click on the "Login" button in the top navigation, then select "Register" to create your free account. You'll need to provide basic information like your name, email, and phone number.

We provide delivery services nationwide, with same-day delivery available in major metropolitan areas. Express delivery is available in most cities, and standard delivery covers all areas within the continental United States.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer multiple delivery options: Same-day delivery (within 2-4 hours in major cities), Express delivery (next business day), Standard delivery (2-3 business days), and Scheduled delivery (choose your preferred date and time).

We handle packages from small envelopes to large items up to 150 lbs. Maximum dimensions are 48" x 48" x 48". For oversized or specialty items, please contact our customer service team for custom solutions.

You can track your package using your tracking number on our tracking page, through your account dashboard, or via SMS/email notifications. Real-time updates include pickup, transit, and delivery confirmation.

If no one is available, we'll leave a delivery notice and attempt redelivery the next business day. You can also provide delivery instructions, authorize safe drop-off locations, or schedule a specific delivery time that works for you.

Pricing & Payment

Shipping cost is based on package size, weight, distance, and delivery speed. You can get an instant quote using our pricing calculator. We also offer volume discounts for business customers and regular shippers.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and business accounts can pay by ACH transfer or be invoiced monthly for regular shipments.

Yes, we offer full refunds for packages that are significantly delayed due to our error, lost, or damaged. Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the scheduled delivery date.

Business Services

Yes, we offer business accounts with volume discounts, monthly invoicing, API integration, bulk shipping tools, and dedicated account management. Contact our sales team to learn more about business solutions.

Yes, we offer API integration and have plugins for popular e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and BigCommerce. Our technical team can help with custom integrations.

All packages include basic coverage up to $100. Additional insurance is available for high-value items up to $5,000. Insurance costs vary based on declared value and can be added during the booking process.

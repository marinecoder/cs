About CourierDash

Your trusted delivery partner since 2018

Our Story

Founded in 2018, CourierDash began as a small local delivery service with a simple mission: to provide reliable, fast, and secure package delivery that customers can trust.

Today, we've grown to serve over 500 cities nationwide, delivering 50,000+ packages monthly while maintaining our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our technology-driven approach ensures real-time tracking, secure handling, and on-time delivery for every single package we handle.
